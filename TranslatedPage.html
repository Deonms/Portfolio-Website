Voidloop

Voidloop is a mining adventure with roguelite and incremental elements.

You play as a lonely Void Spirit who has wandered too far from home. Use your camp as a hub to prepare for each
descent into ever-changing caves filled with rare loot and dangerous foes. Each dive brings you closer to your home at
bottom of the cave...

Features

Descend through 5 unique cave biomes, each filled with procedurally generated floors, unique hazards, and valuable resources.
Race against time as you push toward floor 20 of each cave to collect powerful gems and find your way back to the void.
Return to camp between runs to trade, craft, and upgrade your spirit with the help of the Blacksmith, Shopkeeper, and Witch
Mine, fight, and loot over 60+ unique items, then progress your journey with 100+ upgrades and 34 powerful skills that make you stronger each run.
